Chicken Alfredo Penne Pasta

This dish is a creamy alfredo pasta packed with tons of flavor! To make this dish you will need milk or cream, mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, herbs (spinach, parsley, etc.), and of course your penne pasta.

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Milk or Cream
  • Spinach
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Penne Pasta
  • Parmesan
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Spices of your choice
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Take a medium pot, fill it a quarter way full of salted water and bring that water to a boil
  2. Once the water is bubbling, add in 2-3 servings of penne pasta and stir occasionally.
  3. While your pasta is boiling, finely dice your onions and garlic before adding them to an oiled medium pan to simmer.
  4. As your vegetables simmer, dice your chicken into small cubes, oil the chicken, and add spices of your choice onto all of your chicken cubes.
  5. Once your vegetables have browned slightly, add your chicken to the pan and cook normally until the internal temperature is above 165 degrees. (Make sure to cook both sides)
  6. Set the chicken and vegetables aside in another plate and add milk or cream and half of your mozzarella cheese to your pan that you just cooked your meat in.
  7. Stir the creamy solution and take your penne pasta and add it to the solution along with a small amount of the pasta water.
  8. As you stir and the consistency gets more creamy, remove the pasta and place it into a casserole dish pan as well as your chicken and vegetables.
  9. Top your casserole dish with the rest of your mozzarella cheese, a pinch of parmesan cheese, and more spices if you would like to as well as a drizzle of olive oil.
  10. Bake your casserole dish in the oven at 350 degrees for 8-12 minutes
  11. Enjoy your creamy chicken alfredo penne pasta!
